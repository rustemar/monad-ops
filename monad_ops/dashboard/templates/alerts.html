About severity levels
critical
fires on assertions, panics, chunk-exhaustion events, reorgs, and retry_pct ≥ 75% (60-block avg). Requires operator attention.
warn
fires on retry_pct ≥ 65%, tip lag > 2 blocks, or disk usage ≥ 85%. Watch-only.
info
informational events (probe recoveries, epoch transitions).
recovered
a previously-fired warn/critical alert has returned to baseline. Paired with the original trigger row.
